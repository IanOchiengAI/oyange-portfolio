Nairobi Noir

Client Vogue Africa

Year 2025

Category Editorial

"We wanted to strip away the color to reveal the raw geometry of the city. To capture the juxtaposition of modern architecture and traditional textures."

Nairobi Noir explores the architectural soul of Kenya's capital through a monochromatic lens. This series strips away the vibrant colors typically associated with African photography, instead focusing on form, shadow, and structure.

The project captures moments where traditional and modern elements intersect, creating visual narratives about Kenya's rapid urbanization while honoring its cultural roots. Each frame tells a story of transition, of a city in constant evolution.

Shot over three months during the rainy season, the natural lighting conditions created dramatic contrasts that emphasize the geometric patterns and textures of Nairobi's built environment.
